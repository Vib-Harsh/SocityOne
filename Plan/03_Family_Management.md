# Family Management

## Overview
This module tracks the individuals residing in the properties defined in the Stay Module.

## Key Features
- **Occupancy Tracking**: For each room/flat, identifying if the original owner is residing there or if it is given on rent to a tenant.
- **Family Details**: Storing details of the primary resident and their family members (name, age, relation, contact info).
- **Vehicle Registration**: Linking vehicles (cars/bikes) to specific families for security gate tracking.
- **Domestic Help/Staff Linking**: Associating maids, drivers, or cooks with specific families for streamlined entry/exit at the gates.
- **Move-in / Move-out Workflows**: Processes for onboarding new families and offboarding leaving residents.
