# Payment Management

## Overview
A financial module handling the collection and tracking of society dues.

## Key Features
- **Maintenance Invoicing**: Automated generation of monthly/quarterly maintenance bills based on flat size or type.
- **Online Payments**: Integration with a payment gateway (e.g., Razorpay, Stripe) for easy payment via UPI, Card, or Netbanking.
- **Receipt Generation**: Automatic generation and distribution of payment receipts.
- **Defaulter List**: Admins can easily track unpaid dues and send automated reminders.
- **Expense Tracking**: Admins can log society expenses (paying vendors, staff salaries) to maintain transparent accounting.
