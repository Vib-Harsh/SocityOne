# Contact Directory

## Overview
A secure, internal phonebook for the society.

## Key Features
- **Resident Directory**: A searchable list of all residents, allowing families to connect with neighbors.
- **Privacy Controls**: Residents can choose to hide their phone numbers or only show them to connected friends.
- **Emergency Contacts**: Quick access to society guards, local police, fire station, and nearby hospitals.
- **Management Contacts**: Easy access to the society committee members' contact info.
