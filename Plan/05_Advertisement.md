# Advertisement Module

## Overview
A monetization and communication channel allowing the display of promotional content within the mobile app.

## Key Features
- **Admin Ad Control**: Society admins can upload and schedule advertisements.
- **Ad Placements**: Defining where ads appear (e.g., home screen banners, notice board section).
- **Vendor Ads**: Local businesses (grocery stores, salons) can partner with the society to display ads.
- **Internal Promotions**: Promoting internal society services (e.g., yoga classes hosted by a resident).
- **Analytics**: Basic tracking of ad views or clicks.
