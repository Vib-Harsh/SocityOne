# Complaints Section

## Overview
A ticketing system designed to quickly resolve resident grievances and maintenance requests.

## Key Features
- **Ticket Creation**: Residents can raise complaints (e.g., plumbing, electrical, hygiene) with photos and descriptions.
- **Categorization**: Routing complaints to the correct department or vendor automatically.
- **Status Tracking**: Tracking the lifecycle of a complaint (Open, In Progress, Resolved, Reopened).
- **Admin Dashboard**: Admins can view all complaints, assign them to staff, and track resolution times.
- **Feedback/Rating**: Residents can rate the service after a complaint is resolved.
